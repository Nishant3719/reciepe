Good Old Fashioned Pancake

Ingredients

  1. 1 1/2 cups of all of all purpose flour
  2. 3 1/2 teaspoons of baking powdeer
  3. 1 teaspoon salt
  4. 1 tablespoon of white sugar
  5. 1 1/4 cups milk
  6. 1 egg

Steps

Step1

In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, salt and sugar. make a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg and melted butter; mix until smooth.

Step2

Heat a ligthly oiled girddle or frying pan over medium- high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the girddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.

Step3

Your pancake will tell you when it's ready to flip. Wait until bubbles start to form on the top and the edges look dry and set. This will usually take about two to three minutes on each side. and then Serve Hot
