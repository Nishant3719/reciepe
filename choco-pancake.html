Chocolate Chip Pancakes

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4cups milk
  • 2 large eggs, at room temperature
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup semisweet chocolate chips
  • cooking spray

Steps

Step1

Whisk milk, eggs, and vanilla together in a bowl. Stir in melted butter.

Step2

Whisk flour, cocoa powder, sugar, and salt together in a separate bowl. Pour in wet ingredients and mix until just combined. Stir in chocolate chips.

Step3

Spray a large skillet with cooking spray and heat over medium-high heat. Working in batches, pour 1/4 cup batter into the hot skillet for each pancake. Cook until bubbles form and pop in the center of each pancake, 2 to 3 minutes. Flip and cook until golden brown on the other side, about 1 minute. Serve Hot.
